Me
Data privacy consultant by day. Beer connoisseur by night. Computer programmer by trade. Designer by passion.

I started as a software engineer. But soon realized it wasn't the place for me. I love code, but I didn't love coding as my job; it is more of a hobby. After college, I found myself working for Deloitte in the cyber practice focused on data privacy. To my surprise, it is the perfect balance of technical and strategic. Yet, I find myself lacking a creative outlet. That's where this website comes in.

If I had to describe my brain in three words... open, goofy, minimalistic (in a good way, I think haha)

I recently started writing as a way to finesse my craft and crystallize my thinking. I don't write about one topic, but rather whatever is on my mind at the time.

Technlogy & Design

As a computer science graduate with a minor in visual communication, I am interested in the intersection between technology and design.

The weaving of modern tech with digital design is a relatively new concept. Considering everyday people were able to create and digitally design themselves using a computer starting around the 1980s when programming languages took off, popularizing design tools, browsers, and software languages such as Adobe, Google Chrome, Microsoft Internet Explorer, and C++. And it's seen even more in the past couple years where jobs like data visializer, product designer, blockchain smart contract creator, and VR designer are popping up in mainstream culture. Yet, as a new space these two continue to be inevitably connected.

And in my mind, what connects these two? Most often technology is created to solve a unique problem for group of users. The success of the technology solution is likely dependent on the design. The design needs to address the users needs in form and function. We can't have useful technology without design.

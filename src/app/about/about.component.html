About Me.

Hi there.

My background consists of a computer science degree with a visual communications minor. I started as a software engineer, but soon realized it wasn't the place for me. I love to code, but I didn't love coding as my job; it was more of a hobby.

That's how I ended up in my next gig, Deloitte Cyber where I've been there the past 4 years. I started out in privacy compliance, working with a big tech company maturing an enterprise wide privacy programs to comply with external regulators and supporting a regulatory audit. Next, I pivoted to application security, leading a team that performed the operations of a data protection assessment program inside the company's DevOps function.

Favorite parts about my job:
1) articulating technical information into an easy and digestible format
2) supporting my team by providing subject matter expertise on information security topic, including vulnerability testing, patch management, encryption methods, orgnaization controls such as data loss prevention, full disk encryption, etc., MFA, access controls, and much more.
